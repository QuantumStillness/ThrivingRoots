<idea>
**Design Movement**: Modernist Brutalism
**Core Principles**:
1. **Raw Authenticity**: Unapologetic use of large typography and high contrast.
2. **Structural Clarity**: Grid lines are visible or implied strongly, creating a sense of order.
3. **Content First**: The art itself is the hero; the interface recedes but frames it boldly.
4. **Directness**: Interactions are immediate and snappy, no unnecessary flourishes.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **Monochrome Dominance**: Pure Black (#000000) and Pure White (#FFFFFF) are 95% of the palette.
- **Intent**: To create a gallery-like atmosphere where the colorful (or B&W) art pops. It feels serious, curated, and high-end.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Asymmetric Grid**: Instead of a centered container, use a split-screen or modular grid that varies by section.
- **Sticky Elements**: Product details stick while images scroll.

**Signature Elements**:
- **Oversized Typography**: Headings that break the grid or span the full width.
- **Visible Borders**: Thin 1px lines separating sections, reminiscent of architectural blueprints.
- **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text for announcements or brand values.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Hover Reveals**: Images invert or show a secondary view on hover.
- **Cursor Effects**: A custom cursor (e.g., a large dot or crosshair) that reacts to clickable elements.

**Animation**:
- **Staggered Reveal**: Elements slide up with a hard cut or very fast ease.
- **Parallax**: Subtle parallax on large images to give depth without softness.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: 'Space Grotesk' or 'Syne' (Bold, Uppercase) - for a contemporary, slightly quirky edge.
- **Body**: 'Inter' or 'DM Sans' (Regular) - for absolute legibility.
</idea>

<idea>
**Design Movement**: Soft Minimalism (Swiss Style influenced)
**Core Principles**:
1. **Breathing Room**: Extensive whitespace to let the art "breathe".
2. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight define structure, not lines or boxes.
3. **Fluidity**: Everything feels connected and smooth.
4. **Understated Luxury**: The lack of clutter signals premium quality.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **Off-White & Charcoal**: Instead of harsh black/white, use #FAFAFA (Off-White) and #1A1A1A (Charcoal).
- **Intent**: To feel warmer and more inviting, like a high-end home decor magazine.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Central Axis with Offsets**: Content is generally centered but images break the column to the left or right.
- **Masonry Grids**: For collections, mimicking a gallery wall.

**Signature Elements**:
- **Large Serif Headings**: Elegant, editorial-style typography.
- **Floating Cards**: Products sit on white cards with very soft, diffuse shadows.
- **Overlapping Elements**: Text slightly overlapping images to create depth.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Smooth Drifts**: Hovering lifts elements slightly (Y-axis translation).
- **Soft Fades**: Transitions are slow and graceful.

**Animation**:
- **Fade & Scale**: Images scale up slightly and fade in as they enter the viewport.
- **Smooth Scroll**: A sense of weight in the scrolling experience.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display' or 'Cormorant Garamond' (Italic accents) - for elegance.
- **Body**: 'Lato' or 'Mulish' - friendly and clean.
</idea>

<idea>
**Design Movement**: Digital Bauhaus
**Core Principles**:
1. **Form Follows Function**: Every element has a purpose.
2. **Geometric Precision**: Circles, squares, and triangles are used as decorative yet functional elements.
3. **Dynamic Balance**: Asymmetry that feels perfectly weighted.
4. **Experimental**: Pushing the boundaries of standard e-commerce layouts.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **High Contrast B&W + 1 Accent**: Black, White, and a single electric accent (e.g., Neon Lime or Electric Blue) used *very* sparingly for CTAs.
- **Intent**: To feel cutting-edge, digital-native, and energetic.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Modular Blocks**: The site feels like a set of building blocks.
- **Horizontal Scroll**: Some sections (like "New Arrivals") scroll horizontally to break the vertical rhythm.

**Signature Elements**:
- **Geometric Shapes**: A circle containing "New" or a sale percentage.
- **Grid Lines**: Subtle background grids.
- **Split Headers**: Navigation split across corners.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Micro-interactions**: Buttons change shape or fill on hover.
- **Magnetic Buttons**: Buttons that stick slightly to the cursor.

**Animation**:
- **Geometric Transitions**: Page loads with a wipe or shape reveal.
- **Kinetic Type**: Text that moves or changes weight on scroll.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: 'Archivo' or 'Unbounded' - wide and geometric.
- **Body**: 'Roboto Mono' or 'Space Mono' - technical and precise.
</idea>
